numbers¶

ABCMeta Metaclass for defining Abstract Base Classes (ABCs).
Complex Complex defines the operations that work on the builtin complex type.
Integral Integral adds a conversion to long and the bit-string operations.
Number All numbers inherit from this class.
Rational .numerator and .denominator should be in lowest terms.
Real To Complex, Real adds the operations that work on real numbers.
